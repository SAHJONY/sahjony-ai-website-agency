      5. Term & cancellation

      This Agreement begins on the Effective Date and continues on a month-to-month basis with the hosting/care plan. Either party may cancel with fourteen (14) days’ written notice. Upon Client’s written request and payment of any balance due, Provider will provide an export of the Client’s website content and captured leads.

      Client owns its own brand assets, text, photos, videos, audio, menu/pricing, and captured customer leads. Upon full payment of the build fee, Client owns the final delivered website design for its own use. Provider retains ownership of its underlying platform, templates, code, and AI systems, and grants Client a license to use them for the duration of this Agreement. Client represents that it has the rights to all materials it provides and uploads.
