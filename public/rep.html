💎 What you're selling
You sell local businesses a complete online presence, built by AI, live in days:
  • A stunning website — custom design, mobile, bilingual, on Google, with their photos/menu/services.
  • Ava, an AI receptionist — answers customers 24/7, books appointments, captures every lead (chat, or a real phone line).
  • A client dashboard — they update prices, post promos, and see their leads themselves.
  • Social Autopilot (add-on) — we set up + run their social media and auto-post their promos.
Their cost is near zero to us — the AI builds it — so there's room to work with every budget.
💵 Prices (know these cold)
Websites: Starter $899 · Full site $1,299 (your default — quote first) · Premium $1,999.
Care plan (monthly): $49 / $89 / $149 — hosting, updates, support. Push every deal to include the monthly.
Ava receptionist: Chat $49/mo · Voice line $149/mo.
Social Autopilot: $79/mo + $99 setup.
Payment plans welcome — most clients pay by Zelle or Cash App (card also available).
🎯 How to sell it (5 steps)
  1. Open with them: "I love this place — do many people find you online?" Let them name the problem.
  2. Show, don't pitch: build a free preview of THEIR site on the spot at frontdeskagents.com/builder.html (or send your referral link). Seeing their own shop on screen sells it.
  3. Anchor the middle: "Most shops like yours go with the full site at $1,299 — everything included, live this week."
  4. Attach the monthly + Ava: "For a bit each month I keep it updated and Ava answers your calls 24/7 — you never miss a customer."
  5. Tiny yes: "Want me to send you a free mockup today?" Free mockup → approval → you pass it to us to close & build.
Share your referral link everywhere — sales from it are credited to you automatically.
🛡️ Handle the 4 objections
"Too expensive." → "It's a one-time cost less than a month of ads, working 24/7. Or we start at $899."
"I have a Facebook page." → "Perfect — your site catches the people who Google you, and you own it. We link them."
"No time." → "That's the point — you do nothing. Give me your hours + a few photos, it's live this week."
"Let me think." → "Totally — let me send a free mockup so you have something real to look at. No cost."
