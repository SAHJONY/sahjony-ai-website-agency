01 — Cold call

The phone script

Goal of the call is NOT to sell — it's to earn a “yes” to a free mockup. Smile, slow down, sound like a neighbor.

Opening (first 10 seconds) 

Hi, is this {Owner}? Hey {Owner}, my name's {YourName} — I'm local, here in {City}. I'll be quick, I promise. I help {businessType} in {City} get more customers from Google with a clean little website. Real quick — when someone searches "{businessType} near me," does your place show up with a site, or not really?

The value (after they answer) 

Got it. So here's the thing — most folks check you out online before they ever walk in. If there's no site, or it looks old, they quietly go to the competitor that has one. I build modern, mobile, bilingual sites for {City} businesses — the kind that turns a Google search into someone walking through your door.

The close — ask for the mockup, not the sale 

Tell you what — no cost, no commitment: let me build you a free mockup of what your site could look like. I'll text it to you by tomorrow. If you love it, we make it live this week. If not, no hard feelings. What's the best number to text it to?

Then immediately: “And what's the name you want on it — just {Business}? Perfect. Talk tomorrow.” Hang up. You won.

ES — Llamada en español 

Hola, ¿habla {Owner}? Soy {YourName}, soy de aquí de {City}. Le robo solo un minuto. Ayudo a negocios como el suyo a salir en Google y conseguir más clientes con una página web bonita y moderna. ¿Su negocio ya sale en internet cuando alguien lo busca? — Mire, sin costo y sin compromiso: déjeme hacerle una muestra gratis de cómo se vería su página. Se la mando por mensaje mañana. Si le gusta, la activamos esta semana. ¿A qué número se la envío?
02 — Voicemail

When they don't pick up

Short, warm, and end with a reason to call back. Always follow a voicemail with a text (see Texts).

Voicemail (15 seconds) 

Hi {Owner}, it's {YourName} here in {City} — I build websites for local {businessType}. I actually started putting together a free sample for {Business} and wanted to show you. No cost at all. I'll text it over too. Give me a ring back at {YourPhone} whenever. Thanks!
03 — Walk-in / in person

The highest close rate

Walk in with the mockup already on your phone. Be a customer first, salesperson second.

The in-person opener 

Hey, are you {Owner}? Love this place. I'm {YourName}, I'm local — I build websites for businesses here in {City}. I actually made a quick sample for {Business}, can I show you something on my phone real quick? (Show it.) This could be live this week. Most shops like yours go with the full site — want me to set it up for you?

If the owner isn't in 

No worries! Could you give {Owner} this? (Hand a card.) Tell them {YourName} stopped by with a free website sample for {Business} — it's already built, just needs a yes. I'll follow up tomorrow. Thank you!
04 — Text / SMS sequence

The follow-up that closes

Texts get read. Space these out: mockup same day, nudge +2 days, last call +4 days.

1 · First touch / after a call 

Hi {Owner}, {YourName} here (we spoke about a website for {Business}). Building your free mockup now — I'll send the link tomorrow. 👍

2 · Mockup ready 

{Owner} — here's your free website mockup for {Business}: {link} 🎉 Built it mobile + ready for Google. If you like it I can make it live this week for {price}. Want me to?

3 · Gentle nudge (+2 days) 

Hey {Owner}, did the site come through ok? Happy to tweak the colors or photos — it's your shop, want it perfect. Just say the word and it's live. 🙌

4 · Last call (+4 days) 

{Owner}, I'll hold your mockup a couple more days before I free up the slot. No pressure — just don't want {Business} losing customers to the shop down the street that's already online. Want me to launch it?

ES — Mensaje con la muestra 

{Owner}, aquí está la muestra gratis de la página de {Business}: {link} 🎉 Lista para celular y para Google. Si le gusta, la pongo en línea esta semana por {price}. ¿La activamos?

After they reply

TikTok / DM cold opener

08 — Objection handling

Answers to every "no"

Agree, reframe, redirect to the free mockup. Never argue.

"It's too expensive" 

Totally hear you. It's a one-time {price} — less than a month of ads — and it works 24/7 bringing people in. And if budget's tight we have a simpler version at $899. Want me to show you the free mockup first so you can decide on something real?

"I already have a Facebook page" 

Perfect — and we'll link them. But a website catches the people who Google you and never check Facebook, and you actually own it. Facebook can change the rules anytime; your site is yours forever. Let me show you the mockup, it pulls right alongside your page.

"I don't have time" 

That's exactly why this works — you do nothing. Send me your hours and a couple photos, I build everything, and it's live this week. Honestly takes you 2 minutes.

"Let me think about it" 

Of course. Let me make it easy — I'll send the free mockup so you've got something real to think about, not just an idea. If it's a no after that, no worries at all. Cool?

"I had a guy who never finished it" 

Ugh, I hear that a lot — that's the worst. The difference here: I build it in front of you today, you see it before you pay a dollar, and the monthly means I keep it running. No disappearing act.
09 — Close & get paid

From yes to money

Make paying easy and immediate. Always attach the monthly care plan — that's your recurring profit.

The assumptive close 

Love it — let's get {Business} live. It's {price} to build and launch, then $89/mo for hosting, updates and care so you never touch it. I'll send a payment link now and we'll have you online by {day}. Sound good?

Attach the monthly (the real money) 

The $89/mo is what keeps it working for you — hosting, security, and I update your hours, prices, photos and specials whenever you want. Most owners say it's the best $89 they spend because they never have to think about it. I'll set that up with the build.

Send the invoice / payment link 

Here's your secure payment link for {Business}: {paymentLink}

• Website build & launch: {price}
• Care plan: $89/mo (starts at launch, cancel anytime)

Once that's in I'll publish today and send you the live link. 🎉 Thank you, {Owner}!

Deposit option (for bigger builds) 

We can do half now, half at launch if that's easier — {halfPrice} to start, {halfPrice} when you approve it live. Want me to send the deposit link?
10 — After the sale

Keep them, grow referrals

A launched client is your best lead source. Deliver, then ask.

Launch / handover 

🎉 {Business} is LIVE: {link}

Save it, share it, put it on your Google, Instagram, and door. I'll keep it updated — text me anytime you want to change hours, prices, photos or add a promo. Welcome aboard, {Owner}!

Ask for the referral 

So glad you love it, {Owner}! Quick favor — who's one other business owner you know who could use a site like this? If they sign up, I'll knock a free month of care off your plan. 🙌

Win-back (old lead, never closed) 

Hey {Owner}, {YourName} here. I still have that mockup for {Business} saved. I'm doing a {City} special this month — want me to make it live before it's gone? Takes a day.

Monthly check-in (retention) 

Hi {Owner}! Anything you want updated on the site this month — new photos, a holiday promo, prices? Send it over and I'll have it up today. 👍
